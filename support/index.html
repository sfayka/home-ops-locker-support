Current Release

The current release is local-first. Records and attachments stay on your device unless you choose to export or share them through iOS.

iCloud sync, shared household access, and backend email receipt forwarding are not enabled in this release.

Purchases

Home Ops Locker uses Apple's In-App Purchase system for Premium. Premium unlocks unlimited item records, OCR extraction, advanced exports, and insurance inventory export.

Viewing, editing, deleting, searching, backing up, and exporting existing records remain available after a free user reaches the free item creation limit.

Privacy

Home Ops Locker does not use developer-operated analytics, advertising SDKs, account registration, or backend data storage in the current release.
