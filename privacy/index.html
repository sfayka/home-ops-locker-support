Home Ops Locker Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 18, 2026.

Data Collection

Home Ops Locker is designed as a local-first iOS app for storing home ownership records, receipts, warranties, manuals, serial numbers, reminders, and support packets.

The current Home Ops Locker app does not collect data from the app. Records and attachments you add are stored locally on your device unless you choose to share or export them.

Home Ops Locker does not include developer-operated analytics, advertising SDKs, account registration, or backend data storage in the current release.

User-Controlled Sharing And Exports

You may choose to export or share records, support packet PDFs, backups, CSV files, insurance inventories, QR labels, or attachments. Those actions are initiated by you through iOS sharing or export controls.

If you send exported files to another person, service, repair provider, insurer, or cloud storage provider, that sharing is handled by the destination you choose.

Apple Services

Purchases, subscriptions, TestFlight, App Store distribution, crash reporting, and other Apple platform services are handled by Apple under Apple's terms and privacy policies.

iCloud Sync

The current release is local-first. iCloud sync is not enabled in the current App Store submission.
